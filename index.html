About Me

I used to be a fire engineer, where I learned how to work alone and part of a team. I have worked on multi-million pound projects and I presented talks at international conferences. I became Team Leader, and achieved Chartership status.

I started coding to solve the problems I identified in the industry, automating and introducing efficiency to my team. In learning how to code, I nurtured my passion for programming. I now use my software engineering skills to provide practical solutions.
